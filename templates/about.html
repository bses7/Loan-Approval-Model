How We Make Predictions

At Loan Sense, we leverage state-of-the-art machine learning models to predict loan approvals. Our approach involves the following steps:

  1. Data Collection: We gather relevant data, including applicant details, financial history, and other features that influence loan decisions.
  2. Data Processing: Our team cleans and preprocesses the data to ensure accuracy and reliability.
  3. Model Training: We train various machine learning models such as XGBoost, Random Forest, and Decision Trees to identify patterns and predict loan outcomes.
  4. Prediction: Once trained, our models analyze new loan applications and provide predictions based on learned patterns.
